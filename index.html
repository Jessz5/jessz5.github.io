About Me

Airbnb Dashboard

2023

Airbnb globally connects travelers with short-term rental hosts. Stakeholders can employ insights from the NYC Airbnb dashboard for investment decisions and pricing strategies. Based on the analysis, profitable investment prospects are found in both Staten Island and Manhattan. Manhattan, boasting prime location, tourist demand, and diverse listings, emerges as a standout for maximizing returns, while Staten Island's limited availability propels higher prices.
