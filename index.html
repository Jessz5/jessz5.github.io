NYS Influenza Analysis

2024

Performed exploratory data analysis on New York State's influenza outbreak data for the 2023-2024 season, using Python and Pandas in Google Colab for dataset cleaning. Addressed data integrity in two key datasets containing 4,836 cases and 33,430 hospitalizations by resolving missing values and duplicates, and filtered and merged the datasets into a cohesive set of 2,672 rows for in-depth analysis. Developed an interactive Tableau dashboard to illustrate influenza incidence rates, showcasing trends in cases and hospitalizations across various age groups, regions, and counties.

Airbnb Dashboard

2023

Created a Tableau dashboard to visualize New York City Airbnb listings data, providing insights into pricing trends, popular neighborhoods, and investment opportunities. The dashboard enables hosts and stakeholders to make informed decisions, with profitable prospects identified in both Staten Island and Manhattan. Manhattan stands out for its prime location, tourist demand, and diverse listings, while Staten Island commands higher prices due to its limited availability.
